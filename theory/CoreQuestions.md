# Core Research Questions

> Questions are organized by level of abstraction.
> Higher levels represent meta-questions about lower levels.

---

## Level 0 — Observation

- Why do certain patterns appear in Karnaugh maps?
- Why do Boolean functions of specific types generate recurring visual forms?

---

## Level 1 — Structure

- Why do humans recognize these patterns as *structures* rather than noise?
- What distinguishes a pattern from a structure in human perception?

---

## Level 2 — Discovery

- Why do humans discover particular structures?
- What determines which structures attract attention?

---

## Level 3 — Significance

- Why do certain structures feel important?
- Why do some structures pass the significance filter while others do not?

---

## Level 4 — Question Generation

- Why do certain structures develop into research questions?
- What distinguishes a structure that generates curiosity from one that does not?

---

## Level 5 — Explanation

- How do humans detect explanatory significance?
- Do humans anticipate the existence of an explanation before finding it?

---

## Full Question Inventory

### Level 1 Questions

**Q1.** Why do humans discover structures?

**Q2.** Why do humans perceive certain structures as non-random?

**Q3.** Why do humans judge certain phenomena as meaningful?

**Q4.** Where do research questions come from?

**Q5.** How do humans and AI collaborate in the structure discovery process?

### Level 2 Questions

**Q6.** Does something analogous to intuition exist in AI?

**Q7.** Does AI discover structures, or does it generate them?

**Q8.** Are structure discovery and meaning attribution the same process?

**Q9.** What kind of person is a researcher?

**Q10.** Will the future role of humans shift from computation toward structure discovery?
