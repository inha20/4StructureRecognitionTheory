## Evolution of Research Questions

### Stage 1: Pattern

**Research question:**
> Why do these shapes appear?

**Examples:**
- Checkerboard Pattern
- Ring Pattern
- Corner Pattern

---

### Stage 2: Structure

**Research question:**
> Is this a simple shape, or a deeper structure?

**Examples:**
- Hamming Weight Layer
- Symmetry
- Invariance

---

### Stage 3: Structure Discovery

**Research question:**
> Why do humans discover these structures?

**Examples:**
- Visual recognition of repetition
- Recognition of symmetry
- Recognition of invariance

---

### Stage 4: Research Generation

**Research question:**
> Why do certain structures become research questions?

**Examples:**
- Anomalous observations
- Difference from expectation
- Unexplained phenomena

---

### Stage 5: Human-AI Collaboration

**Research question:**
> What roles do humans and AI respectively play in the structure discovery process?

---

## Hierarchy of Research Questions

```
Level 0: Observation
    ↓
Level 1: Structure
    ↓
Level 2: Discovery
    ↓
Level 3: Significance
    ↓
Level 4: Question Generation
    ↓
Level 5: Explanation
```

Each level raises a meta-question about the level below it.
Structure Recognition Theory operates primarily at Levels 2–5.
