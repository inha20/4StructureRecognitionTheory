# Attention Selection Hypothesis

Status: Exploratory Hypothesis

Date: 2026-06-03

---

## Central Question

Why do some patterns attract attention while others are ignored?

---

## Observation

Humans encounter countless patterns every day.

Only a small fraction become the focus of conscious attention.

This suggests the existence of an attention-selection mechanism.

---

## Working Hypothesis

Attention acts as a cognitive filter.

Pattern
→ Attention Selection
→ Structure Discovery

Without attention, structure discovery may never occur.

---

## Candidate Attention-Capturing Features

Possible features include:

- Novelty
- Symmetry
- Repetition
- Contrast
- Anomaly
- Surprise
- Invariance
- Unexpected regularity

---

## Attention and Research

Research may begin only after a phenomenon passes through attention selection.

Attention therefore acts as the first bottleneck in the research-generation process.

---

## Relationship to Structure Recognition Theory

Structure Recognition Theory proposes:

Difference
→ Attention
→ Structure
→ Meaning
→ Question
→ Research

This hypothesis focuses on the attention stage.

---

- How does expertise influence attention?
