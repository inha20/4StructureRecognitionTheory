# Concept Genealogy

Status: Exploratory Framework

Date: 2026-06-03

---

## Central Question

How do concepts evolve and generate new concepts?

---

## Motivation

Research progress may not be driven solely by the accumulation of facts.

New concepts often make previously unseen structures visible.

These concepts may then generate additional observations, questions, and concepts.

---

## Example Genealogy

Multiplication Structure
→ Function Structure
→ Pascal-Genetics Correspondence
→ Symmetric Functions
→ Karnaugh Map Patterns
→ Variable Rearrangement Invariance
→ Structure Discovery
→ Research Worthiness
→ Concept as Lens
→ Structure Recognition Theory

---

## Working Hypothesis

Concepts may function as lenses.

A new concept can:

- Compress observations
- Reveal hidden relationships
- Generate new questions
- Enable new research directions

---

## Concept Evolution Cycle

Phenomenon
→ Concept
→ Expanded Perception
→ New Observation
→ New Concept

This process may repeat recursively.

---

## Relationship to Structure Recognition Theory

Structure Recognition Theory may be viewed as the current stage in an ongoing concept genealogy.

Future concepts may emerge from it and extend the research program.
