## Concept as Lens

Concepts may function as observation tools.

Examples:

- XOR reveals checkerboard structures.
- Symmetry reveals hidden relationships.
- Invariance reveals persistent structure under transformation.

A concept does not merely explain a phenomenon.

A concept may reveal phenomena that were previously invisible.
