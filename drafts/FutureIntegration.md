## Concept as Lens

Current Structure Recognition Theory focuses on how humans discover structures and generate research questions.

A possible extension is that concepts themselves may function as lenses.

A concept may not merely explain an observation.

A concept may make new observations possible.

Examples:

- XOR reveals checkerboard-like Boolean structures.
- Invariance reveals relationships between transformed representations.
- Symmetry reveals common structure among apparently different patterns.
