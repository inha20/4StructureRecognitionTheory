# Hypotheses vNext

## Purpose

This document proposes candidate future hypotheses that may extend the current hypothesis framework.

These hypotheses should be treated as exploratory and not yet integrated into the main theory.

---

## H8: Concept as Lens Hypothesis

Concepts do not merely explain observations.

Concepts may function as lenses that reveal previously unnoticed structures.

A change in concept may produce a change in observation.

---

## H9: Concept Evolution Hypothesis

Research does not end with explanation.

Research may generate concepts.

Those concepts may enable new observations, producing additional research.

Possible cycle:

Research
↓
Concept
↓
Expanded Perception
↓
New Observation
↓
New Question
↓
New Research

---

## H10: Generative Concept Hypothesis

Some concepts generate more future research than others.

A concept may be valuable not only because it explains phenomena, but because it generates new observations, questions, and theories.

---

## Candidate Evaluation Criteria for Concepts

- Compression
- Generativity
- Predictive Power
- Transferability
- Visibility Enhancement

---

## Relationship to Existing Hypotheses

H8-H10 are intended as possible extensions to:

- Structure Discoverer Hypothesis
- Representation Transformation Hypothesis
- Research Worthiness Hypothesis

These hypotheses may connect structure discovery with concept evolution and scientific progress.
