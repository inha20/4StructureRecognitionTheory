## Proposed Higher-Level Question

How do humans come to see something new?

## Candidate New Questions

### Concept Questions

- What makes a concept good?
- Are concepts discovered or invented?
- Why do some concepts generate new research programs?

### Observation Questions

- How do concepts change perception?
- Why do some concepts reveal previously invisible structures?
- Can new observations be produced by conceptual change alone?

### Research Growth Questions

- What causes a research field to expand?
- How do concepts generate further concepts?
- Can concept evolution be modeled systematically?
