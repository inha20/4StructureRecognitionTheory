## Notes

This document summarizes the current conceptual architecture of Structure Recognition Theory.

The model proposes that research generation and concept evolution are interconnected processes.

Research produces concepts.
Concepts expand perception.
Expanded perception enables new observations.
New observations generate new research questions.
