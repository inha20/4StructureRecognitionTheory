## Long-Term Expansion (2027+)

### Potential New Research Lines

1. **Formal Structural Invariance Theory**  
   Mathematical treatment of structural invariance under permutation groups  
   (Extension of Paper 3 toward formal mathematics)

2. **Computational Structure Discovery**  
   Can AI systems be designed to discover structures that humans then recognize as meaningful?

3. **Significance Filter Formalization**  
   Can H4 (Significance Filter) be operationalized into a computable criterion?

4. **Cross-Domain Structure Recognition**  
   Does the research generation framework apply outside Boolean functions?  
   Candidate domains: number theory, graph theory, music, language

5. **Structure-Based Mathematics Education**  
   Curriculum design: elementary mathematics through structure  
   Target: adult learners who failed traditional approaches

## Evaluation Question for Expansion Decisions

> Does this expansion strengthen the coherence of the research program, or does it fragment it?

When choosing between expansion directions, prefer those that:
1. Connect to existing hypotheses (H1–H7)
2. Generate new empirical observations
3. Extend the program's existing concept genealogy
