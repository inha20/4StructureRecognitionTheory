## Working Title

Structure Recognition Theory:
Toward an Explanation of How Humans Discover New Structures, Generate Questions, and Initiate Research

---

## Abstract

This paper proposes Structure Recognition Theory, a framework for understanding how humans discover structures, assign meaning, generate questions, develop concepts, and initiate research. The theory integrates observations from mathematics, logic, visualization, human cognition, and human-AI collaboration.
