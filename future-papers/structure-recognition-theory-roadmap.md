# Structure Recognition Theory Roadmap

Status: Long-Term Research Roadmap

Date: 2026-06-03

---

## Vision

Develop an integrated theory explaining how humans discover structures, generate questions, create concepts, and initiate research.

---

## Stage 1: Mathematical Case Studies

Completed or ongoing:

- Karnaugh Map Pattern Research
- Symmetric Boolean Functions
- Variable Rearrangement Invariance

Goal:

Identify concrete examples of structure discovery.

---

## Stage 2: Structure Discovery

Questions:

- Why do humans perceive structure?
- Which structural features attract attention?
- What makes a pattern appear non-random?

---

## Stage 3: Research Generation

Proposed process:

Difference
→ Attention
→ Structure
→ Meaning
→ Research Worthiness
→ Question
→ Research

Goal:

Understand how research begins.

---

## Stage 4: Concept Evolution

Proposed process:

Phenomenon
→ Concept
→ Expanded Perception
→ New Observation
→ New Research

Goal:

Understand how concepts drive scientific growth.

---

## Stage 5: Human-AI Collaboration

Questions:

- What are human strengths?
- What are AI strengths?
- How can collaborative discovery be optimized?

---

## Stage 6: AI Collaboration Education

Focus:

- AI usage education
- AI collaboration education
- Human-AI role division

---

## Long-Term Goal

Build a unified framework connecting:

- Structure discovery
- Meaning attribution
- Question generation
- Concept evolution
- Human-AI collaboration

---

## Notes

This roadmap is exploratory and expected to evolve as the research program develops.
